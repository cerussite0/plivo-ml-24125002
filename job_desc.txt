Job Description: 

AI/ML Engineer – Plivo, Bengaluru
About Plivo
Plivo is a global leader in powering customer engagement for some of the world’s largest B2C brands including Uber, WhatsApp and Zomato. Our AI Agents Platform automates the entire customer lifecycle, from acquisition to engagement and support using cutting-edge multimodal AI combining LLMs, speech-to-text, text-to-speech and real-time voice intelligence. We operate at global scale with high-impact products that handle over 1 billion API requests per month. We’re now looking for the brightest, most passionate AI/ML minds to help shape the future of real-time AI systems.

Role Overview:
As an AI/ML Engineer at Plivo, you will design, train, and ship production models that power real-time voice and conversational agents used by customers around the world. You will own problems end to end, from data and modeling through inference pipelines and the latency, accuracy, and reliability of what finally runs in production. The work is hands-on and high-ownership, with real impact from day one, and it sits right where real-time systems meet applied AI.

Key Responsibilities

● Train, fine-tune, and deploy models for speech recognition, speaker isolation,
turn detection, and language understanding across multiple languages and
verticals.
● Build and optimize inference pipelines for real-time, global-scale traffic,
balancing latency, accuracy, and throughput.
● Work on the systems behind our agent platform: orchestration, model evals,
and MCP-based integrations, retrieval, evaluation, and the guardrails that keep
agents reliable in production.
● Research and build low latency models that run at a fraction of the cost via
CPU at a production scale
● Turn large and messy datasets into signals, surfacing the patterns and
improvements that move model performance.
● Partner closely with engineering, product, and data teams to take features from
prototype to production.
● Build internal tooling, evaluate open-source frameworks, and bring in what is
worth adopting.
● Keep pace with progress in LLMs, generative AI, and voice intelligence, and
apply it where it earns its place.
